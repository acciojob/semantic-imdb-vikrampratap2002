Summary

3 Idiots is a story about three engineering students and their journey through college life. The movie highlights friendship, the pressure of education, and the importance of following your passion instead of running after success.

Actors

Main actors in the movie include Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, and Boman Irani.

Favorite Quote

"Success ke peeche mat bhaago, excellence ka peecha karo, success jhak maar ke tumhare peeche aayegi."
